Vitor Prates

Hi, I'm an Architect and Urban Planner who has been working since 2017 on environmental studies, focusing on the impacts of real estate developments on the neighborhood and city in which it'll be built.
After many studies were delivered, I realized that technology would be an excellent ally for quantifying the impacts arising from the construction of real estate projects on urban space. Soon after, I started to learn how to code and manipulate data, which improved the data analised and the quality of impact mitigation proposals.
In my portfolio you can consult some Urban Analytics case studies (with open datasets) and Data Visualization.

Traffic Flow microssimulation

This project considers traffic volume data of an intersection in the city of Maringá, state of Paraná, Brazil, and performs a microsimulation (with Sumo and Infraworks softwares) in order to quantitatively analyze the performance of the intersection considering both softwares. Afterwards, a dashboard is created comparing the results of both softwares.
